# Porting FAQ

## Do you support number porting?

Yes, Wavix supports number porting in 36 countries. US porting is automated via the customer portal. For other countries, contact lnp@wavix.com. Note: porting is only available for Wavix Flex Pro customers.

## How long does porting take?

Porting timelines vary by carrier and country. US ports typically complete in 2-4 weeks. International ports may take longer. Our LNP team will guide you through the process and provide status updates.
