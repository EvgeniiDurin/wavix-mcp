# SIP trunk FAQ

## Is my PBX compatible with Wavix?

Yes, most PBXs and SBCs are compatible with Wavix. We support standard SIP protocol with both digest and IP-based authentication. Popular platforms like Asterisk, FreePBX, 3CX, FreeSwitch integrate seamlessly. Check our integration guides for step-by-step setup instructions.

## Do you support IP authentication?

Yes. Wavix supports SIP trunk IP authentication. You can activate SIP trunk IP authentication using our intuitive GUI. All SIP requests sent from your allowlist, static IP will be automatically authenticated without username/password.

## Do you offer Emergency calling services?

Yes, we support E911/000/111/999/112 in the US, Canada, Australia, New Zealand and most countries in the European Union. Customers can activate the feature using the Wavix API or GUI. This feature is available exclusively on Wavix Flex Pro.

## Do you support TLS and SRTP?

Yes, we support both secure SIP signaling (TLS) and encrypted media streams (SRTP). Contact support to enable encryption on your account.

## How many outbound channels do I get?

When you sign up for Wavix, you're given two outbound channels. If you'd like to increase that outbound capacity, please contact support. For high-volume needs, email sales@wavix.com with details about your use case.

## Do you support automated dialing?

Wavix supports automated dialing where it's permitted. We have years of experience helping customers set up automated dialing campaigns that comply with regulations. Contact sales@wavix.com for guidance.

## Do you provide call recording services?

Yes. Our recording feature is programmable - you can choose which calls to record, retention period, and whether to use dual or single channel recording.

## Do you offer voicemail detection?

Wavix excels in voicemail detection. Our AI-based AMD (Answering Machine Detection) is at least 95% accurate and constantly improving thanks to machine learning.

## Can I use my existing numbers as Caller ID?

Yes, you can bring your own Caller ID. Contact support@wavix.com or file a ticket and we'll help you set it up. Numbers must be verified or ported to your account.

## What are your SIP server addresses?

We have regional gateways for optimal latency:
- nl.wavix.net (Europe)
- us.wavix.net (United States)
- sg.wavix.net (Singapore/Asia)
- au.wavix.net (Australia)

Use the gateway closest to your location for best performance.
