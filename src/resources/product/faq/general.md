# General FAQ

## Where are your points of presence?

Our PoPs are strategically placed for optimal call quality, security, and reliability. We maintain global PoPs across Amsterdam (EU), Dallas (US), Sydney (AU), and Singapore (Asia), and are expanding into Latin America and Africa.

## What are the billing increments?

Wavix Flex customers are billed in one-minute increments. Wavix Flex Pro customers are billed per-second where possible. Contact sales@wavix.com for details.

## How do I get support?

Contact support@wavix.com or use the chat widget in your dashboard. Enterprise customers have dedicated support channels. Documentation is available at docs.wavix.com.
