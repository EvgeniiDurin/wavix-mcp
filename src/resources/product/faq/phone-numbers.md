# Phone numbers FAQ

## What types of numbers do you offer?

We offer:
- Local numbers from thousands of cities worldwide
- National/non-geographic numbers
- Toll-free numbers (US, UK, and more)
- Mobile numbers (SMS-enabled)
- Short codes for high-volume SMS
- Vanity numbers with repeated digits

## Does Wavix provide mobile phone numbers?

Yes. We have tens of thousands of phone numbers across various countries. Choose from numbers in Australia, United States, United Kingdom, Canada, and more. US and Canadian numbers can be SMS-enabled. If you can't find numbers in your region, contact provisioning@wavix.com.

## What documentation is needed to activate a number?

Requirements vary by country. Common documents include: ID (driver's license, passport), proof of residence (utility bill, lease, company incorporation docs). Some countries require a Letter of Intent explaining your business use case.
