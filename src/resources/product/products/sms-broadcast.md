# SMS Broadcast

Mass SMS campaign management platform

## Features

### Segment Calculator

Accurately predict message segments and costs before sending campaigns.

### Message Templates

Create, save, and reuse message templates with dynamic personalization fields.

### Engagement Tracking

Monitor delivery rates, opens, clicks, and responses in real-time.

### Trusted Sender

Build sender reputation with compliant messaging practices and verified numbers.

### Optimal Timing

Schedule messages for optimal delivery times based on recipient timezone.

### Dynamic Content

Personalize messages with recipient data using merge fields and conditional logic.

### List Management

Upload, segment, and manage contact lists with automatic deduplication.

### Opt-out Handling

Automatic processing of opt-out requests to maintain compliance.
