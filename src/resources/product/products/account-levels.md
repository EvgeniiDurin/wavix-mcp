# Account Plans

Flexible pricing plans for different business needs

## Features

### Wavix Flex

Pay-as-you-go pricing with no monthly minimum. Perfect for testing and low-volume use cases.

### Wavix Flex Pro

Committed monthly spend with volume discounts. Best for established businesses with predictable usage.

### Enterprise

Custom pricing and SLAs for high-volume customers. Dedicated support and account management.
