# SMS Messaging

Enterprise SMS messaging platform for global 2-way communications

## Features

### Messaging API

Elegant, logical API to power your global 2-way messaging. RESTful endpoints with comprehensive documentation and SDKs.

### Sender ID Management

Alphanumeric, shortcodes, local or toll-free sender IDs available. Manage multiple sender identities from a single account.

### Guaranteed Delivery

Fast and reliable worldwide delivery. Runs on direct carrier connections for optimal deliverability and speed.

### Opt-out Management

Support for unsubscribe keywords as required by local regulators. Automatic handling of STOP, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, and QUIT keywords.

### Verified Encoding

Tested, assured Unicode compatibility on all partner networks. Full support for international characters and emoji.

### 2-way Communications

Add SMS-enabled numbers from our worldwide number inventory. Receive and respond to inbound messages.

## Use Cases

- Marketing notifications - Plan, design and execute marketing campaigns
- Service alerts - Deliver critical status change notifications
- New lead alerts - Quickly notify sales when new prospects sign up
- Appointment reminders - Help customers manage recurring appointments
- 2FA verifications - Confirm transactions and prevent fraud
- Voting surveys - Build and launch customer voting campaigns
