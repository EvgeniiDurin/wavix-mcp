# Phone Numbers (DID)

Global phone number inventory with SMS, voice, and MMS capabilities

## Features

### Massive Inventory

Search and manage numbers from thousands of cities worldwide. Local, national, toll-free, and mobile numbers available.

### SLA-backed Service

Our own network in multiple countries for guaranteed Quality of Experience. 99.99% uptime SLA.

### Optimized TCO

Better cost controls with a single worldwide number provider. Volume discounts and flexible pricing.

### SMS and MMS Capable

Go beyond voice calls. Add messaging capabilities to your US and Canadian numbers.

### 2-way Messaging

Connect with your audience using short codes or long numbers. Run SMS campaigns and gather reactions.

### Number Porting

Port your numbers globally. Track and manage porting orders through the self-service portal.

### Flexible Routing

Seamlessly integrate your inbound phones. Point numbers to any SIP-capable PBX or forward to external numbers.

### Massive Channel Capacity

Numbers configured for high-throughput capacity, expandable to tens of thousands of concurrent inbound calls.

### Secure and Encrypted

Protect privacy with TLS and SRTP encryption. Ensure safety and confidentiality of your calls.
