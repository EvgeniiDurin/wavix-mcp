AI-powered call analysis for quality control and insights

Identify specific words, phrases, or topics mentioned during calls.

Identify trends, issues, and opportunities to improve customer experience.

Track agent performance, coaching opportunities, and best practices.

Convert speech to text for searchable call records and compliance documentation.
