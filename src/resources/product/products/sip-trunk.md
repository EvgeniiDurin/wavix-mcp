# SIP Trunking

Enterprise-grade SIP trunking for voice communications

## Features

### Seamless SIP-connect

Connect your PBX or contact center to the PSTN through our global SIP infrastructure. Compatible with all major platforms.

### Geographically Redundant

Multiple regional gateways (US, EU, APAC, Australia) for low latency and high availability.

### Ultra-high Quality

Premium voice quality with HD codec support. Direct carrier connections ensure crystal-clear calls.

### Flexible Authentication

Choose between digest (username/password) or IP-based authentication for your trunks.

### Unlimited Channels

Scale from a single line to thousands of concurrent calls. No per-channel fees.

### Real-time Analytics

Monitor call quality, usage, and costs through the dashboard or API.
