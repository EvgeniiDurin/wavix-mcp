# Transportation & Logistics

**Move faster. Track smarter.**

Optimize your transportation and logistics operations with comprehensive communication solutions. From fleet management to customer updates, keep everyone connected.

## Key Features

### Fleet Communication

Maintain constant contact with drivers and vehicles through voice and SMS channels for efficient dispatch and coordination.

### Shipment Tracking

Provide real-time shipment visibility to customers with automated tracking notifications and status updates.

## Capabilities

### Dispatch Automation

Automate dispatch communications with drivers through SMS and voice for route assignments and updates.

### Customer Notifications

Keep shippers and receivers informed with automated pickup and delivery notifications.

### Exception Alerts

Instant alerts for delays, route changes, or issues requiring immediate attention.

### Driver Check-ins

Automated driver check-in calls for safety compliance and status updates.

### Load Matching

SMS-based load board notifications to quickly match available drivers with shipments.

### Documentation Delivery

Send BOL, POD, and other documentation electronically via SMS or email.
