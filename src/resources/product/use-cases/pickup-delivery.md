# Pickup & Delivery

**Connect. Deliver. Delight.**

Streamline your pickup and delivery operations with real-time communication. Keep customers informed, coordinate with drivers, and ensure smooth last-mile delivery.

## Key Features

### Real-time Coordination

Enable instant communication between customers, drivers, and dispatch centers for efficient delivery coordination.

### Automated Notifications

Keep all parties informed with automated status updates, ETA notifications, and delivery confirmations.

## Capabilities

### Order Confirmation

Send instant order confirmations via SMS with order details and estimated delivery times.

### Driver Communication

Enable masked calling between customers and drivers without exposing personal phone numbers.

### Delivery Updates

Automatic notifications when orders are out for delivery, approaching, and delivered.

### Rescheduling Options

Allow customers to reschedule or redirect deliveries through SMS or voice IVR.

### Proof of Delivery

Confirm delivery completion with SMS notifications and electronic signatures.

### Feedback Collection

Gather customer feedback on delivery experience through automated surveys.
