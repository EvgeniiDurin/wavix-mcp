<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up for an account">
    Go to [wavix.com](https://wavix.com/sign-up) and create an account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate your account">
    Confirm your email address and phone number during the sign-up process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get approval">
    Wait for your account to be approved by the Wavix team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose an account level">
    After approval, choose either the Wavix Flex or Flex Pro account level.
    For more information, see the [Account Levels](https://wavix.com/account-levels) page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Need help? Visit the [Wavix Account FAQ](https://wavix.com/faq/account) for answers to common account questions.
</Tip>
